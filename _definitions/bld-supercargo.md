---
title: Supercargo
letter: S
permalink: "/definitions/bld-supercargo.html"
body: An agent of the own-6r of goods shipped as cargo on a vessel, who has charge
  of the cargo on board, sells the same to the best advantage in the foreign market,
  buys a cargo to be brought back on the return voyage of the ship, and comes home
  with it
published_at: '2018-07-07'
source: Black's Law Dictionary 2nd Ed (1910)
layout: post
---
