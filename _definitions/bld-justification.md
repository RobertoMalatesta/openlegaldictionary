---
title: Justification
letter: J
permalink: "/definitions/bld-justification.html"
body: A maintaining or showing a sufficient reason in court why the defeudant did
  what he is called upon to an-swer, particularly in an action of libel. A defense
  of justification is a defense showing the libel to be true, or in an action of assault
  showing the violence to have been necessary. See Steph. Pl. 184
published_at: '2018-07-07'
source: Black's Law Dictionary 2nd Ed (1910)
layout: post
---
