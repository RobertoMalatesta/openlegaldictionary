---
title: Wealth
letter: W
permalink: "/definitions/wealth.html"
body: All material objects, capable of satisfying human wants, desires, or tastes,
  having a value in exchange, and upon which human labor has been expended; i. e.,
  which have, by such labor, been either reclaimed from nature, extracted or gathered
  from the earth or sea, manufactured from raw materials, improved, adapted, or cultivated
published_at: '2018-07-07'
source: Black's Law Dictionary
layout: post
---
