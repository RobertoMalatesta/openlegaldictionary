---
title: Tanto, Right Of
permalink: "/definitions/tanto-right-of.html"
body: In Mexican law. The right enjoyed by nn usufructuary of property, of buying
  the property at the same price at which the owner offers it to any other person,
  or is willing to take from another. Civ. Code Mex. art 992
