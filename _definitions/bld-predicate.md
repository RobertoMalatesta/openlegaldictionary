---
title: Predicate
letter: P
permalink: "/definitions/bld-predicate.html"
body: In logic. That which is said concerning the subject in a logical prop-osition;
  as, “The law is the perfection of common sense." “Perfection of common sense," being
  affirmed concerning the law, (the subject,) is the predicate or thing predicated,
  wharton; Bourland v. Hildreth, 26 Cal. 232
published_at: '2018-07-07'
source: Black's Law Dictionary 2nd Ed (1910)
layout: post
---
