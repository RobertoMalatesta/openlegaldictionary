title: Proprietor
letter: P
permalink: "/definitions/proprietor.html"
body: This term is almost synonymous with “owner,” (q. v.,) as in the phrase “riparian
  proprietor.” A person en-titled to a trade-mark or a design under the acts for the
  registration or patenting of trade-marks and designs (q. v.) is called “proprietor”
  of the trade-mark of design. Sweet See Latham v. Roach, 72 111. 181; Tuengling v.
  Schile (C. C.) 12 Fed. 105; Hunt v. Curry, 37 Ark. 105; werckmelster v. Springer
  Lithographing Co. (C. C.) 63 Fed. 811
