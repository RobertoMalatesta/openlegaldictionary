title: Prairie
letter: P
permalink: "/definitions/bld-prairie.html"
body: An extensive tract of level or rolling land, destitute of trees, covered with
  coarse grass, and usually characterized by a deep, fertile soil, webster. See Buxton
  v. Railroad Co., 58 Mo. 45; Brunell v. Hop-kins, 42 Iowa, 429
