body: Those who, hav-ing been thought dead, had, after a long absence in foreign countries,
  returned safely home; or those wbo, having been thought dead in battle, had afterwards
  unexpectedly escaped from thelr enemies and returned home. These, among the Romans,
  were not permitted to enter their own houses at the door, bnt were received at a
  passage opened in the roof. Enc. Lond
