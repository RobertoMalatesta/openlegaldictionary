---
title: Adversary proceeding
letter: A
permalink: "/definitions/usc-adversary-proceeding.html"
body: A lawsuit arising in or related to a bankruptcy case that begins by filing a
  complaint with the court, that is, a "trial" that takes place within the context
  of a bankruptcy case.
