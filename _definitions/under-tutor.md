---
title: Under-Tutor
letter: U
permalink: "/definitions/under-tutor.html"
body: In Louisiana. In every tutorship there shall be an under-tutor, whom it shall
  be the duty of the judge to appoint at the time letters of tutor-ship are certified
  for the tutor. It is the duty of the under-tutor to act for the minor whenever the
  interest of the minor is in opposition to the interest of the tutor. Civ. Code La.
  1838, arts. 300, 301
