---
title: Bankruptcy administrator
letter: B
permalink: "/definitions/usc-bankruptcy-administrator.html"
body: An officer of the Judiciary serving in the judicial districts of Alabama and
  North Carolina who, like the United States trustee, is responsible for supervising
  the administration of bankruptcy cases, estates, and trustees; monitoring plans
  and disclosure statements; monitoring creditor committees; monitoring fee applications;
  and performing other statutory duties.
