---
title: Under-Lease
letter: U
permalink: "/definitions/under-lease.html"
body: In conveyancing. A lease granted by one who is himself a lessee for years, for
  any fewer or less number of years than he himself holds. If a deed passes all the
  estate or time of the termor, it is au assignment; but, if it be for less portion
  of time than the whole term, it is an under-lease, and leaves a reversion in the
  termor. 4 Kent, Comm. 96
