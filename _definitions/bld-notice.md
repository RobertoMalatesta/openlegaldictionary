---
title: Notice
letter: N
permalink: "/definitions/bld-notice.html"
body: Knowledge; Information; the result of observation, whether by the senses or
  the mind; knowledge of the existence of a fact or state of affairs; the means of
  knowledge. Used in this sense in such phras-es as “A. had notice of the conversion,”
  “a purchaser without notice of fraud,” etc
published_at: '2018-07-07'
source: Black's Law Dictionary 2nd Ed (1910)
layout: post
---
