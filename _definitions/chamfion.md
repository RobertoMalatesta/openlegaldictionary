---
title: Chamfion
letter: C
permalink: "/definitions/chamfion.html"
body: A person who fights a corn-bat in his own cause, or in place of another. The
  person who, in the trial by battel, fought either for the tenant or demandant. 3
  Bl. Comm. 339
