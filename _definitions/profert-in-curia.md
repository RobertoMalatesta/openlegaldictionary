title: Profert In Curia

body: IA. L. Lat. He pro-duces in court. In old practice, these words were inserted
  in a declaration, as an allega-tion that the plaintiff was ready to produce, or
  did actually produce, lu court, the deed or other written instrument on which his
  suit was founded, in order that the court might inspect the same and the defendant
  hear it read. The same formula was used where the defeudant pleaded a written instrument
