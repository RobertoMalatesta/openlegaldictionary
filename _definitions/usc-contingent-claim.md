---
title: Contingent claim
letter: C
permalink: "/definitions/usc-contingent-claim.html"
body: A claim that may be owed by the debtor under certain circumstances, e.g., where
  the debtor is a cosigner on another person's loan and that person fails to pay.
published_at: '2018-08-06'
source: US Courts Glossary
