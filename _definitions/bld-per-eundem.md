---
title: Per Eundem
letter: P
permalink: "/definitions/bld-per-eundem.html"
body: Lat. By the same. This phrase is commonly* used to express “by, or from the
  mouth of, the same judge.” So "per eundem in eadem" means “by the same judge in
  the same case.”
published_at: '2018-07-07'
source: Black's Law Dictionary 2nd Ed (1910)
layout: post
---
