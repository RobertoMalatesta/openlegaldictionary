---
title: Per Proguration
letter: P
permalink: "/definitions/bld-per-proguration.html"
body: By proxy; by one acting as an agent with special powers; as under a letter of
  attorney. These words “give notice to all persons that the agent is acting under
  a special and limited authority." 10 C. B. 689. The phrase is commonly ab-breviated
  to “per proc.,” or “p. p.,” and is more used in the civil law and in England than
  in American law
published_at: '2018-07-07'
source: Black's Law Dictionary 2nd Ed (1910)
layout: post
---
