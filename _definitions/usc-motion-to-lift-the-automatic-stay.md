---
title: Motion to lift the automatic stay
letter: M
permalink: "/definitions/usc-motion-to-lift-the-automatic-stay.html"
body: A request by a creditor to allow the creditor to take action against the debtor
  or the debtor's property that would otherwise be prohibited by the automatic stay.
