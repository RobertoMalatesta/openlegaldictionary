---
title: Poursuivant
letter: P
permalink: "/definitions/bld-poursuivant.html"
body: The king’s messenger; a royal or state messenger. In the heralds* college, a
  functionary of lower rank than a herald, but discharging similar duties, called
  also “poursuivant at arms.”

source: Black's Law Dictionary 2nd Ed (1910)
