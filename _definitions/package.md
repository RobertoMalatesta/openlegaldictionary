body: A package means a bundle put up for transportation or commercial handling; a
  thing in form to become, as such, an article of merchandise or delivery from hand
  to hand. A parcel is a small package; “parcel” being the diminutive of “package.”
  Each of the words denotes a thing in form suitable for transportation or handling,
  or sale from hand to hand. U
