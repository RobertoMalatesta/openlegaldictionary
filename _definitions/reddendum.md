---
title: Reddendum
letter: R
permalink: "/definitions/reddendum.html"
body: Lat. In conveyancing. Rendering; yielding. The technlcal name of that clause
  in a conveyance by whlch the grantor creates or reserves some new thing to himself,
  out of what he had before grant-ed; as "rendering therefor yearly the sum of ten
  shillings, or a pepper-corn,” etc. That clause in a lease in which a rent is reserved
  to the lessor, and which commences with the word "yielding” 2 Bl. Comm. 299
published_at: '2018-07-07'
source: Black's Law Dictionary
