---
title: Jestimatio Capitis
letter: J
permalink: "/definitions/jestimatio-capitis.html"
body: In Saxon law. The estimation or valuation of the head; the price or value of
  a man. By the laws of Athelstan, the life of every man not except-ing that of the
  king himself, was estimated at a certaln price, which was called the were, or cestimatio
  capitis. Crabb, Eng. Law, c. 4
