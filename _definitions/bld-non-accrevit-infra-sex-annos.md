title: Non Accrevit Infra Sex Annos
letter: N
permalink: "/definitions/bld-non-accrevit-infra-sex-annos.html"
body: It did not accrue within six years. The name of a plea by which the defendant
  sets up the statute of limitations against a cause of action which is barred after
  six years.
