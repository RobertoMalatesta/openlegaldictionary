---
title: Vendee
letter: V
permalink: "/definitions/bld-vendee.html"
body: A purchaser or buyer; one to whom anything is sold. Generally used of the transferee
  of real property, one who acquires chattels by sale being called a “buy-er.”
published_at: '2018-07-07'
source: Black's Law Dictionary 2nd Ed (1910)
