---
title: Executory contracts
letter: E
permalink: "/definitions/usc-executory-contracts.html"
body: Contracts or leases under which both parties to the agreement have duties remaining
  to be performed. If a contract or lease is executory, a debtor may assume it (keep
  the contract) or reject it (terminate the contract).
published_at: '2018-08-06'
source: US Courts Glossary
layout: post
---
