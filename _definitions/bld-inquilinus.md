---
title: Inquilinus
letter: I
permalink: "/definitions/bld-inquilinus.html"
body: In Roman law. A ten-ant; one who hires and occupies another's house; but particularly,
  a tenant of a hired house in a city, as distinguished from colo-nus, the hirer of
  a house or estate in the country. Calvin
published_at: '2018-07-07'
source: Black's Law Dictionary 2nd Ed (1910)
