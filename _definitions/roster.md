title: Roster
letter: R
permalink: "/definitions/roster.html"
body: A list of persons who are to perform certain legal duties when called upon in
  -their turn. In military affairs it is a table or plan by which the duty of officers
  is reg-ulated. See Matthews v. Bowman, 25 Me. 167
