---
title: Reaffirmation agreement
letter: R
permalink: "/definitions/usc-reaffirmation-agreement.html"
body: An agreement by a debtor to continue paying a dischargeable debt after the bankruptcy,
  usually for the purpose of keeping collateral or mortgaged property that would otherwise
  be subject to repossession.
published_at: '2018-08-06'
source: US Courts Glossary
layout: post
---
