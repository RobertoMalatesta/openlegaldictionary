---
title: Inference
letter: I
permalink: "/definitions/bld-inference.html"
body: In the law of evidence. A truth or proposition drawn from another which is supposed
  or admitted to be true. A process of reasoning by which a fact or proposition sought
  to be established Ib deduced as a logical consequence from other facts, or a state
  of facts, already proved or admitted. Gates v. Hughes, 44 wis. 336; Whitehouse v.
  Bolster, 95 Me. 458, 50 Ati. 240; Joske v. Irvine, 91 Tex. 574, 44 S. W. 1059
published_at: '2018-07-07'
source: Black's Law Dictionary 2nd Ed (1910)
