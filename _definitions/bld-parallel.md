---
title: Parallel
letter: P
permalink: "/definitions/bld-parallel.html"
body: For two lines of street railway to be “parallel,” within the meaning of a statute,
  it may not be necessary that the two lines should be parallel for the whole length
  of each or either route. Ex-act parallelism is not contemplated. Cron-in v. Highland
  St Ry. Co., 144 Mass. 254, 10 N. E. 833. And see East St. Louis Connecting Ry. Co.
  v. Jarvis, 92 Fed. 735, 34 C. C. A. 639; Louisville & N. R. Co. v. Ken-tucky, 161
  U. 8. 677, 16 Sup. Ct 714, 40 L. Ed. 849
published_at: '2018-07-07'
source: Black's Law Dictionary 2nd Ed (1910)
