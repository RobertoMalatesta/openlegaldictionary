---
title: Plan
letter: P
permalink: "/definitions/bld-plan.html"
body: A map, chart, or design; being a delineation or projection on a plane sur-face
  of the ground lines of a house, farm, street, city, etc., reduced in absolute length,
  but preserving their relative positions and proportion. Jenney v. Des Moines, 103
  Iowa, 347, 72 N. W. 550; wetherill v. Pennsylvania R. Co., 195 Pa. 156, 45 Atl.
  658u
