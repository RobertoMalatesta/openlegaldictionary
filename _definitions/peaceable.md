---
title: Peaceable
permalink: "/definitions/peaceable.html"
body: Free from the character of force, violence, or trespass; as, a “peace-able entry”
  on lands. "Peaceable posses-sion” of real estate is such as is acquiesced in by
  all other persons, including rival claimants, and not disturbed by any forcible
  at-tempt at ouster nor by adverse suits to re-cover the possession or the estate.
  See Stanley v. Schwalby, 147 U. S. 508, 13 Sup. Ct. 418, 37 L. Ed. 259; Allaire
  v. Ketcham, 55 N. J. Eq. 168, 35 Atl. 900; Bowers v. Cherokee Bob, 45 Cal. 504;
  Gltten v. Lowry, 15 Ga. 336
published_at: '2018-07-07'
layout: post
