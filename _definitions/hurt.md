title: Hurt
letter: H
permalink: "/definitions/hurt.html"
body: In such phrases as “to the hurt or annoyance of another,” or “hurt, molest-ed,
  or restrained in his person or estate," this word is not restricted to physical
  in-juries, but Includes also mental pain, as well as discomfort or annoyance. See
  Row-land v. Miller (Super. N. Y.) 15 N. Y. Supp. 702; Pronk v. Brooklyn Heights
  R. Co., 68 App. Div. 390, 74 N. T. Supp. 375; Thurston v. whitney, 2 Cush. (Mass.)
  110
