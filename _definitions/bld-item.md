title: Item
letter: I
permalink: "/definitions/bld-item.html"
body: Also; likewise; again. This word was formerly used to mark the beginning of
  a new paragraph or division after the first, whence is derived the common application
  of it to denote a separate or distinct particular of an account or bill. See Horwitz
  v. Norris, 60 Pa. 282; Baldwin v. Morgan, 73 Miss. 276, 18 South. 919
