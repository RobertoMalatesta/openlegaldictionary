title: Recommendation
letter: R
permalink: "/definitions/bld-recommendation.html"
body: In feudal law. A method of converting allodial land into feudal property. The
  owner of the allod surrendered it to the king or a lord, doing homage, and received
  it back as a benefice or feud, to hold to hlmself aud such of hls heirs as he had
  previously nominated to the superior
