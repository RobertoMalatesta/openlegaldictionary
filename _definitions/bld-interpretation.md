title: Interpretation
letter: I
permalink: "/definitions/bld-interpretation.html"
body: The art or process of discovering and expounding the intended signification
  of the language used lu a statute, will, contract, or any other written document,
  that is, the meaning which the author designed it to convey to others. People v.
  Com’rs of Taxes, 95 N. Y. 559; Rome ▼. Knox, 14 How. Prac. (N. Y.) 272; Ming v.
  Pratt, 22 Mont 262, 56 Pac. 279; Tallman v. Tallman, 3 Mlsc. Rep. 465, 23 N. Y.
  Supp. 734
