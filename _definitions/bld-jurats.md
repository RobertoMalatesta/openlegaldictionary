---
title: Jurats
letter: J
permalink: "/definitions/bld-jurats.html"
body: In English law. officers in the nature of aldermen, sworn for the government
  of many corporations. The twelve assistants of the bailiff in Jersey are called
  “jurats.”
published_at: '2018-07-07'
source: Black's Law Dictionary 2nd Ed (1910)
