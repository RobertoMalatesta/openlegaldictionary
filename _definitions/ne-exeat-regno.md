---
title: Ne Exeat Regno
letter: N
permalink: "/definitions/ne-exeat-regno.html"
body: Lat. In English practice. A writ which issues to restrain a person from leaving
  the kingdom. It was formerly used for political purposes, but is now only resorted
  to in equity when the de-fendant is about to leave the kingdom; it is only In cases
  where the intention of the par-ty to leave can be shown that the writ is granted
