title: Quinto Exactus
letter: Q
permalink: "/definitions/quinto-exactus.html"
body: In old practice. Called or exacted the fifth tlme. A return made by the sheriff,
  after a defendant had been proclaimed, required, or exacted in five county courts
  successively, and falled to appear, upon which he was outlawed by the coroners of
  the county. 3 BL Comm. 283
