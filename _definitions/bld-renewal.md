title: Renewal

permalink: "/definitions/bld-renewal.html"
body: The act of renewlng or reviving. The substitution of a new grant, engagement,
  or right, in place of one which has expired, of the snme character and on the same
  terms and conditions as before; as, the renewal of a note, a lease, a patent. See
  Carter v. Brooklyn L. Ins. Co., 110 N. Y. 15, 17 N. E. 396; Gault v. McGrath, 32
  Pa. 392; Kedey v. Petty. 153 Ind. 179, 54 N. E. 798; Pltts v. Hall, 19 Fed. Cas.
  758
