---
title: Redemption
letter: R
permalink: "/definitions/usc-redemption.html"
body: A procedure in a Chapter 7 case whereby a debtor removes a secured creditor's
  lien on collateral by paying the creditor the value of the property. The debtor
  may then retain the property.
published_at: '2018-08-06'
source: US Courts Glossary
layout: post
---
