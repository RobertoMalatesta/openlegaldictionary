body: To assign to a bidder at an auction by a knock or blow of the hammer. Property
  is‘ said to he “knocked down” when the auctioneer, by the fall of his hammer, or
  by any other audible or visl-ble announcement, signifies to the bidder that he is
  entitled to the property on paying the amount of his bid, according to the terms
  of the sale. “Knocked down” and “struck off” are synonymous terms. Sherwood v. Reade,
  7 Hill (N. ¥.) 439
