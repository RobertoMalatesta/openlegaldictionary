title: Bum-Bailiff
letter: B
permalink: "/definitions/bld-bum-bailiff.html"
body: A person employed to dun one for a debt; a bailiff employed to ar-rest a debtor.
  Probably a vulgar corruption of “bound-bailiff,” (q. r.)
