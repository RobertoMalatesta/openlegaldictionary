---
title: Prebankruptcy planning
letter: P
permalink: "/definitions/usc-prebankruptcy-planning.html"
body: The arrangement (or rearrangement) of a debtor's property to allow the debtor
  to take maximum advantage of exemptions. (Prebankruptcy planning typically includes
  converting nonexempt assets into exempt assets.)
published_at: '2018-08-06'
source: US Courts Glossary
layout: post
---
