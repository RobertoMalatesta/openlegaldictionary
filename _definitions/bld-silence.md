---
title: Silence
letter: S
permalink: "/definitions/bld-silence.html"
body: CE. The state of a person who does not speak, or of one who refrains from speaking.
  In the law of estoppel, “silence” impiles knowledge and an opportunity to act upon
  it. Pence v. Langdon, 99 U. S. 581, 25 L. Ed. 420; Stewart v. wyoming Cattle Ranch
  Co., 128 U. S. 383, 9 Sup. Ct. 101, 32 L. Ed. 439; Chicora Fertilizer Co. v. Dunan,
  91 Md. 144, 46 Atl. 347, 50 L. R. A. 401
published_at: '2018-07-07'
source: Black's Law Dictionary 2nd Ed (1910)
layout: post
