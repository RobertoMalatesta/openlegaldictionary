---
title: Pretrial conference
letter: P
permalink: "/definitions/usc-pretrial-conference.html"
body: A meeting of the judge and lawyers to plan the trial, to discuss which matters
  should be presented to the jury, to review proposed evidence and witnesses, and
  to set a trial schedule. Typically, the judge and the parties also discuss the possibility
  of settlement of the case.
published_at: '2018-08-06'
source: US Courts Glossary
