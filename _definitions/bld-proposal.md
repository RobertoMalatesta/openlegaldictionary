---
title: Proposal
letter: P
permalink: "/definitions/bld-proposal.html"
body: An offer; something prof-fered. An offer, by one person to another, of terms
  and conditions with reference to some work or undertaking, or for the trans-fer
  of property, the acceptance whereof will make a contract between them. Eppes v.
  Mississippi, G. A T. R. Co., 35 Ala. 33
published_at: '2018-07-07'
source: Black's Law Dictionary 2nd Ed (1910)
