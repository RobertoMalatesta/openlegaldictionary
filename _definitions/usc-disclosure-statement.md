---
title: Disclosure statement
letter: D
permalink: "/definitions/usc-disclosure-statement.html"
body: A written document prepared by the chapter 11 debtor or other plan proponent
  that is designed to provide "adequate information" to creditors to enable them to
  evaluate the chapter 11 plan of reorganization.
published_at: '2018-08-06'
source: US Courts Glossary
layout: post
---
