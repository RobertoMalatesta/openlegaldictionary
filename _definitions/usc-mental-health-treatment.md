---
title: Mental health treatment
letter: M
permalink: "/definitions/usc-mental-health-treatment.html"
body: Special condition the court imposes to require an individual to undergo evaluation
  and treatment for a mental disorder. Treatment may include psychiatric, psychological,
  and sex offense-specific evaluations, inpatient or outpatient counseling, and medication.
