---
title: Mechanic
letter: M
permalink: "/definitions/bld-mechanic.html"
body: A workman employed in shaping and uniting materials, such as wood, metal, etc.,
  into some kind of structure, machine, or other object, requiring the use of tools.
  Story v. walker. 11 Lea (Tenn.) 517, 47 Am. Rep. 303; In re osborn (D. C.) 104 Fed.
  781; Savannah & C. R. Co. v. Callahan, 49 Ga. 511; Berks County v. Bertolet, 13
  Pa. 524
published_at: '2018-07-07'
source: Black's Law Dictionary 2nd Ed (1910)
layout: post
---
