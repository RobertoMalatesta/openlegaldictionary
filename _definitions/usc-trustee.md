---
title: Trustee
letter: T
permalink: "/definitions/usc-trustee.html"
body: The representative of the bankruptcy estate who exercises statutory powers,
  principally for the benefit of the unsecured creditors, under the general supervision
  of the court and the direct supervision of the U.S. trustee or bankruptcy administrator.
  The trustee is a private individual or corporation appointed in all chapter 7, chapter
  12, and chapter 13 cases and some chapter 11 cases. The trustee's responsibilities
  include reviewing the debtor's petition and schedules and bringing actions against
  creditors or the debtor to recover property of the bankruptcy estate. In chapter
  7, the trustee liquidates property of the estate, and makes distributions to creditors.
