---
title: Statu Liberi
letter: S
permalink: "/definitions/bld-statu-liberi.html"
body: Lat in Louisiana. Slaves for a time, who had acquired the right of being free
  at a time to come, or on a condition which was not fulfilled, or in a certain event
  which had not happened, but who in the mean time remained in a state of slavery.
  Clv. Code La. (Ed. 1838) art. 37
published_at: '2018-07-07'
source: Black's Law Dictionary 2nd Ed (1910)
layout: post
---
