title: In Facie Ecclesls
letter: I
permalink: "/definitions/bld-in-facie-ecclesls.html"
body: In the face of the church. A term applied In the law of England to marriages,
  which are required to be solemnized in a parish church or public chapel, unless
  by dispensation or license. 1 Bl. Comm. 439; 2 Steph. Comm. 288, 289. Applied in
  Bracton to tiie old mode of conferring dower. Bract fol. 92; 2 Bl. Comm. 133
published_at: '2018-07-07'
source: Black's Law Dictionary 2nd Ed (1910)
