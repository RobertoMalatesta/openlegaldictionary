---
title: Functional Disease
letter: F
permalink: "/definitions/functional-disease.html"
body: In medical jurisprudence, one which prevents, ob-structs, or interferes with
  the due perforin-ance of its special functions by any organ of the body, without
  anatomical defect or ab-normality in the organ itself. See Higbee v. Guardian Mut.
  L. Ins. Co., 66 Barb. (N. Y.) 472. Distinguished from “organic” disease, which is
  due to some injury to, or lesion or malformation in, the organ in question
published_at: '2018-07-07'
source: Black's Law Dictionary
layout: post
