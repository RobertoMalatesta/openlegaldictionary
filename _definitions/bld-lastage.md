---
title: Lastage
letter: L
permalink: "/definitions/bld-lastage.html"
body: A custom exacted in some fairs and markets to carry things bought whither one
  will. But it is more accurately taken for the ballast or lading of a ship. Also
  custom paid for wares sold by the last, as herrings, pitch, etc. wharton
published_at: '2018-07-07'
source: Black's Law Dictionary 2nd Ed (1910)
layout: post
---
