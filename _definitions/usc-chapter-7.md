---
title: Chapter 7
letter: C
permalink: "/definitions/usc-chapter-7.html"
body: The chapter of the Bankruptcy Code providing for "liquidation," that is, the
  sale of a debtor's nonexempt property and the distribution of the proceeds to creditors.
  In order to be eligible for Chapter 7, the debtor must satisfy a "means test." The
  court will evaluate the debtor's income and expenses to determine if the debtor
  may proceed under Chapter 7.
