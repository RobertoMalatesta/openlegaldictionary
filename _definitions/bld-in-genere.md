---
title: In Genere
letter: I
permalink: "/definitions/bld-in-genere.html"
body: In kind; in the same genus or class; the same in quantity and quality, but not
  individually the same. In the Roman law, things which may be given or restored in
  genere are distinguished from such as must be given or restored in specie; that
  is, identically. Mackeld. Rom. Law, | 16L
published_at: '2018-07-07'
source: Black's Law Dictionary 2nd Ed (1910)
layout: post
