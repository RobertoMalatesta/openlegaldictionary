---
title: U.S. attorney
letter: U
permalink: "/definitions/usc-us-attorney.html"
body: A lawyer appointed by the President in each judicial district to prosecute and
  defend cases for the federal government. The U.S. Attorney employs a staff of Assistant
  U.S. Attorneys who appear as the government's attorneys in individual cases.
published_at: '2018-08-06'
source: US Courts Glossary
