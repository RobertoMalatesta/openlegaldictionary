---
title: Deposition
letter: D
permalink: "/definitions/usc-deposition.html"
body: An oral statement made before an officer authorized by law to administer oaths.
  Such statements are often taken to examine potential witnesses, to obtain discovery,
  or to be used later in trial. See discovery.
published_at: '2018-08-06'
source: US Courts Glossary
layout: post
---
