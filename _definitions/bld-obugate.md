---
title: Obugate
letter: O
permalink: "/definitions/bld-obugate.html"
body: To bind or constrain; to bind to the observance or performance of a duty ;,
  to place under an obligation. To bind one's self by an obligation or promise; to
  as* surae a duty; to execute a written promise or covenant; to make a writing obligatory.
  Wachter v. Famachon, 62 wis. 117, 22 N. W. 160; Maxwell v. Jacksonville Loan & Imp.
  Co., 45 Fla. 425, 34 South. 255
published_at: '2018-07-07'
source: Black's Law Dictionary 2nd Ed (1910)
layout: post
---
