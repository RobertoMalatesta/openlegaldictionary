letter: I
permalink: "/definitions/bld-incumbrance.html"
body: Any right to, or ln-terest in, land which may subsist in third persons, to the
  diminution of the value of the es-tate of the tenant, but consistently with the
  passing of the fee. Fitch v. Seymour, 9 Metc
