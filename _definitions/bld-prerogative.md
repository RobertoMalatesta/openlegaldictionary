---
title: Prerogative
letter: P
permalink: "/definitions/bld-prerogative.html"
body: An exclusive or peculiar privilege. The special power, privilege, immunity,
  or advantage vested in an offi-cial person, either generally, or in respect to the
  things of his office, or in an official body, as a court or legislature. See Attorney
  General v. Blossom, 1 wis. 817; Attorney General v. Eau Claire, 37 wis. 443
published_at: '2018-07-07'
source: Black's Law Dictionary 2nd Ed (1910)
