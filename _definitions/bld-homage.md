---
title: Homage
letter: H
permalink: "/definitions/bld-homage.html"
body: 'In feudal law. A service (or the ceremony of rendering it) which a tenant was
  bound to perform to his lord on receiving investiture of a fee, or succeeding to
  it as heir, in acknowledgment of the ten-ure. It is described by Littleton as thc
  most honorable service of reverence that a free tenant might do to his lord. The
  ceremony was as follows: The tenant, heing ungirt and wlth hare head, knelt before
  the lord'
published_at: '2018-07-07'
source: Black's Law Dictionary 2nd Ed (1910)
