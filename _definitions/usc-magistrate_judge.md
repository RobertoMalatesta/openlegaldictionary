---
title: Magistrate judge
letter: M
permalink: "/definitions/usc-magistrate_judge.html"
body: A judicial officer of a district court who conducts initial proceedings in criminal
  cases, decides criminal misdemeanor cases, conducts many pretrial civil and criminal
  matters on behalf of district judges, and decides civil cases with the consent of
  the parties.
published_at: '2018-08-06'
source: US Courts Glossary
