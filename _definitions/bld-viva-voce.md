---
title: Viva Voce
letter: V
permalink: "/definitions/bld-viva-voce.html"
body: Lat with the living voice; by word of mouth. As applied to the examination of
  witnesses, this phrase ts equivalent to “orally.” It is used in contra-distinction
  to evidence on affidavits or depo-sitlons. As descriptive of a species of voting,
  it signifies voting by speech or outcry, as dis-tinguished from voting by a written
  or print-ed ballot
published_at: '2018-07-07'
source: Black's Law Dictionary 2nd Ed (1910)
layout: post
