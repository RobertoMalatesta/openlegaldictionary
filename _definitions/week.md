title: Week

body: A period of seven consecutive days of time; and, in some uses, the period beginning
  with Sunday and ending with Snt-urday. See Leach v. Burr, 188 U. S. 510, 23 Sup.
  CL 393, 47 L. Ed. 567; Ronkendorff v. Taylor, 4 Pet 361, 7 L. Ed. 882; Evans v.
  Job, 8 Nev. 324; Bird v. Burgsteiner, 100 Ga. 486, 28 S. E. 219; Steinle v. Bell,
  12 Abb. Prac. N. S. (N. Y.) 175; Russell v. Croy, 164 Mo. 69, 63 S. W. 849; Medland
  v. Linton, 60 Neb. 249, 82 N. W. 866
