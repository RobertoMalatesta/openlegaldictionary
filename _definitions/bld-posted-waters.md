body: In Vermont Waters flowing through or lying upon Inclosed or cultivated lands,
  which are preserved for the exclusive use of the owner or occupant by hls posting
  notices (according to the stat-ute) prohibiting all persons from shooting, trapping,
  or fishing thereon, nnder a prescrib-ed penalty. See State v. Theriault, 70 Vt.
  617, 41 Ati. 1030, 43 L. R. A. 290. 67 Am. St. Rep. 695
