---
title: Hearsay
letter: H
permalink: "/definitions/usc-hearsay.html"
body: Evidence presented by a witness who did not see or hear the incident in question
  but heard about it from someone else. With some exceptions, hearsay generally is
  not admissible as evidence at trial
published_at: '2018-08-06'
source: US Courts Glossary
