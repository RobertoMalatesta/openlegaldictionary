---
title: Power Of Appointment
permalink: "/definitions/power-of-appointment.html"
body: A pow-er or authority conferred by one person by deed or will upon another (called
  the “do-nee”) to appoint, that is, to select and nominate, the person or persons
  who are to receive and enjoy an estate or an income there-from or from a fund, after
  the testator’s death, or the donee's death, or after the termination of an existing
  right or interest. See Heinemann v. De wolf, 25 R. I. 243, 55 AU. 707
published_at: '2018-07-07'
layout: post
---
