---
title: Language
letter: L
permalink: "/definitions/language.html"
body: Any means of conveying or communicating ideas; specifically, human speech, or
  the expression of ideas by written characters. The letter, or grammatical import,
  of a document or instrument, as distiu-guished from its spirit; as “the language
  of the statute.” See Behling v. Btate, 110 Ga. 754, 36 8. E. 85; Steveuson v. State,
  90 Ga. 456, 16 S. E. 95; Cavan v. Brooklyn (City Ct. Brook.) 5 N. I. Supp. 759
published_at: '2018-07-07'
source: Black's Law Dictionary
layout: post
---
