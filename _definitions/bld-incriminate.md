---
title: Incriminate
letter: I
permalink: "/definitions/bld-incriminate.html"
body: To charge with crime; to expose to an accusation or charge of crime; to involve
  oneself or another in a criminal prosecution or the danger thereof; as, iu the rale
  that a witness is not bound to five testimony which would tend to incriminate him
published_at: '2018-07-07'
source: Black's Law Dictionary 2nd Ed (1910)
layout: post
---
