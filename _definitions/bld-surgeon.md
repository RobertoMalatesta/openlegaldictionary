---
title: Surgeon
letter: S
permalink: "/definitions/bld-surgeon.html"
body: one whose profession or occupation is to cure diseases or injuries of the body
  by manual operation; one whose occupation is to cure local Injuries or disorders,
  whether by manual operation, or by medication and , constitutional treatment, webster.
  See Smith v. Laue, 24 Hun (N. Y.) 632; Stewart v. Raab, 55 Minn. 20, 56 N. W. 256-;
  Nelson v. State Board of Health, 108 Ky. 769, 57 S. W. 501, 50 L. R. A. 383.
