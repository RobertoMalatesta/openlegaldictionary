---
title: Probation
letter: P
permalink: "/definitions/usc-probation.html"
body: Sentencing option in the federal courts. With probation, instead of sending
  an individual to prison, the court releases the person to the community and orders
  him or her to complete a period of supervision monitored by a U.S. probation officer
  and to abide by certain conditions.
