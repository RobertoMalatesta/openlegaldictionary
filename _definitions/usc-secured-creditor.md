---
title: Secured creditor
letter: S
permalink: "/definitions/usc-secured-creditor.html"
body: A secured creditor is an individual or business that holds a claim against the
  debtor that is secured by a lien on property of the estate. The property subject
  to the lien is the secured creditor's collateral.
published_at: '2018-08-06'
source: US Courts Glossary
layout: post
