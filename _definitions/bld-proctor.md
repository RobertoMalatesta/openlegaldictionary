title: Proctor
letter: P
permalink: "/definitions/bld-proctor.html"
body: A procurator, proxy, or attorney. More particularly, an officer of the admiralty
  and ecclesiastical courts whose duties and business correspond exactly to tbose
  of an attorney at law or solicitor in chancery
