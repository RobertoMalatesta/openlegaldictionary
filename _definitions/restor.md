title: Restor
letter: R
permalink: "/definitions/restor.html"
body: R, or RESTOR. The remedy or recourse which marine underwriters have against
  each other, according to the date of their assurances, or against the master, if
  the loss arise through his default, as through ill loading, want of caulking, or
  want of hav-Ing the vessel tight; also the remedy or re-course a person has against
  his guarantor or other person who is to indemnify him from any damage sustained.
  Enc. Lond

source: Black's Law Dictionary
