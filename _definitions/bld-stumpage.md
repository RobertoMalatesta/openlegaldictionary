---
title: Stumpage
letter: S
permalink: "/definitions/bld-stumpage.html"
body: The sum agreed to be paid to an owner of land for trees stauding (or lying)
  upon his land, the purchaser being permitted to enter upon tiie land and to cut
  down and remove the trees; in other words, It is the price paid for a license to
  cut Blood v. Drummond, 67 Me. 478
published_at: '2018-07-07'
source: Black's Law Dictionary 2nd Ed (1910)
layout: post
---
