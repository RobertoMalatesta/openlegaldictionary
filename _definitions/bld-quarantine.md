---
title: Quarantine
letter: Q
permalink: "/definitions/bld-quarantine.html"
body: A period of time (theoretically forty days) during which a vessel, coming from
  a place where a contagious or Infectious disease is prevalent, is detained by authority
  in the harbor of her port of destl-nation, or at a station near it, without being
  permitted to land or to discharge her crew or passengers. Quarantine is said to
  have been first established at Venice in 1484. Baker, Quar. 3
published_at: '2018-07-07'
source: Black's Law Dictionary 2nd Ed (1910)
layout: post
---
