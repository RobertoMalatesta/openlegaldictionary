body: A conveyance for letters or dis-patches. The word is derived from “positi,”
  the horses carrying the letters or dispatches being kept or placed at fixed stations.
  The word is also applied to the person who conveys the letters to the houses where
  he takes up and lays down hls charge, and to the stages or distances between house
  and house. Hence the phrases, post-boy, post-horse, post-house, etc. wharton
