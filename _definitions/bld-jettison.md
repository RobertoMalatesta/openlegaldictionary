---
title: Jettison
letter: J
permalink: "/definitions/bld-jettison.html"
body: The act of throwing over-board from a vessel part of the cargo, in case of extreme
  danger, to lighten the ship. The same name is also given to the thing or things
  so cast out. Gray v. wain, 2 Serg. & R. (Pa.) 254, 7 Am. Dec. 642; Butler v. wildman,
  3 Barn. & Aid. 326; Barnard v. Adams, 10 How. 303, 13 L. Ed. 417
published_at: '2018-07-07'
source: Black's Law Dictionary 2nd Ed (1910)
layout: post
---
