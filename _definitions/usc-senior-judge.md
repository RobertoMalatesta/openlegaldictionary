---
title: Senior judge
letter: S
permalink: "/definitions/usc-senior-judge.html"
body: A federal judge who, after attaining the requisite age and length of judicial
  experience, takes senior status, thus creating a vacancy among a court's active
  judges. A senior judge retains the judicial office and may cut back his or her workload
  by as much as 75 percent, but many opt to keep a larger caseload.
published_at: '2018-08-06'
source: US Courts Glossary

---
