title: Quarterone
letter: Q
permalink: "/definitions/bld-quarterone.html"
body: In the Spanish and French west Indies, a quadroon, that is, a person one of
  whose parents was white and the other a mulatto. See Daniel v. Guy, 19 Ark. 131.

source: Black's Law Dictionary 2nd Ed (1910)
