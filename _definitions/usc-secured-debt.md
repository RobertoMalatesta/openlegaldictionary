---
title: Secured debt
letter: S
permalink: "/definitions/usc-secured-debt.html"
body: Debt backed by a mortgage, pledge of collateral, or other lien; debt for which
  the creditor has the right to pursue specific pledged property upon default. Examples
  include home mortgages, auto loans and tax liens.
published_at: '2018-08-06'
source: US Courts Glossary
layout: post
---
