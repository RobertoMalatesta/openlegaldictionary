---
title: Plead
letter: P
permalink: "/definitions/plead.html"
body: To make, deliver, or file any pleading; to conduct the pleadings in a cause.
  To interpose any pleading in a suit which contalns allegations of fact; in this
  sense the word is the antithesis of “demur." More particularly, to deliver in a
  formal manner the defendant’s answer to the plain-tiff's declaration, or to the
  indictment, as the case may be

source: Black's Law Dictionary
