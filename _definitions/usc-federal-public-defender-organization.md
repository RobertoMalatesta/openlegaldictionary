---
title: Federal public defender organization
letter: F
permalink: "/definitions/usc-federal-public-defender-organization.html"
body: As provided for in the Criminal Justice Act, an organization established within
  a federal judicial circuit to represent criminal defendants who cannot afford an
  adequate defense. Each organization is supervised by a federal public defender appointed
  by the court of appeals for the circuit.
published_at: '2018-08-06'
source: US Courts Glossary
