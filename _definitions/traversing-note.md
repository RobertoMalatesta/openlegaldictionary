---
title: Traversing Note
letter: T
permalink: "/definitions/traversing-note.html"
body: This is a pleading in chancery, and consists of a denial put in by the plaintiff
  on behalf of the defendant, generally denying all the statements in the plaintiff's
  bill The effect of it is to put the plaintiff upon proof of the whole contents of
  his bill, and is only resorted to for the pur-pose of saving titue, and in a case
  where the plaintiff can safely dispense with an answer. A copy of the note must
  he served on the defendant Brown
published_at: '2018-07-07'
source: Black's Law Dictionary
