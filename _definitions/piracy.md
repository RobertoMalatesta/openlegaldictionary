title: Piracy
permalink: "/definitions/piracy.html"
body: In criminal law. A robbery or forcible depredation on the high seas, without
  lawful authority, done animo furandi, in the spirit and Inteutloh of universal hostility.
  United States v. Palmer, 3 wheat. 610, 4 L. Ed. 471. This is the definition of this
  offense by the law of nations. 1 Kent; Cotnm. 183. And see Talbot v. Janson, 3 Dall.
  152, 1 L. Ed. 540; Dole v. Insurance Co., 51 Me. 467; U. S. v. Smith, 5 wheat 161,
  5 L. Ed. 57; U. S. v. The Ambrose Light (D. C.) 25 Fed. 408; Davison v. Seal-skins,
  7 Fed. Css. 192
