title: Due process
letter: D
permalink: "/definitions/usc-due-process.html"
body: In criminal law, the constitutional guarantee that a defendant will receive
  a fair and impartial trial. In civil law, the legal rights of someone who confronts
  an adverse action threatening liberty or property.
