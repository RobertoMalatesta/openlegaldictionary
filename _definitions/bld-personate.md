---
title: Personate
letter: P
permalink: "/definitions/bld-personate.html"
body: In criminal law. To as-sume the person (character) of another, wlth-out his
  consent or knowledge, in order to de-ceive others, and, in such feigned character,
  to fraudulently do some act or gain some advantage, to the harm or prejudice of
  the person counterfeited. See 2 East, P. C. 1010
published_at: '2018-07-07'
source: Black's Law Dictionary 2nd Ed (1910)
