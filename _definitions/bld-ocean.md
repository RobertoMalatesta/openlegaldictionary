---
title: Ocean
letter: O
permalink: "/definitions/bld-ocean.html"
body: The main or open sea; the high sea; that portion of the sea which does not lie
  within the body of any country and is not subject to the territorial jurisdiction
  or control of any country, but is open, free, and common, to the use of all nations.
  See U. S. v. Rodgers, 150 U. S. 249. 14 Sup. Ct. 109, 87 L. Ed. 1071; D. S. v. New
  Bed-ford Bridge, 27 Fed. Cas. 120; De Lovio v. Boit, 7 Fed. Cas. 428; U. S. v. Morel,
  26 Fed. Gas. 1312
published_at: '2018-07-07'
source: Black's Law Dictionary 2nd Ed (1910)
layout: post
---
