title: Right Of Representation And Performance

body: By the acts 3 & 4 Wm. IV. c. 15, and 5 & 6 VicL c. 45, the author of a play,
  opera, or musical composition, or his assignee, has the sole right of repre-senting
  or causing it to*be represented in public at any place ln the British dominions
  during the same period as the copyright in the work exists. The right ls distinct
  from the copyright, and requires to be separately Registered. Sweet
