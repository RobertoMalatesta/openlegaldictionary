title: Vulgaris Purgatio
letter: V
permalink: "/definitions/vulgaris-purgatio.html"
body: Lat in old English law. Common purgation; a name given to the trial by ordeal,
  to distinguish it from the canonical purgation, which was by the oath of the party.
  4 Bl. Comm. 342
