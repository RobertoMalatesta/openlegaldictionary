---
title: Successor
letter: S
permalink: "/definitions/bld-successor.html"
body: one who succeeds to the rights or the place of another; particularly, the person
  or persons who constitute a corporation after the death or removal of those who
  preceded them as corporators
published_at: '2018-07-07'
source: Black's Law Dictionary 2nd Ed (1910)
layout: post
---
