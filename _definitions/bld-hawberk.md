---
title: Hawberk
letter: H
permalink: "/definitions/bld-hawberk.html"
body: A coat or shirt of mail; hence, derivatively (in feudal law) one who held a
  fief on the duty or service of providing himself with such armor and standing ready,
  thus equipped, for military service when called on. wharton

source: Black's Law Dictionary 2nd Ed (1910)
