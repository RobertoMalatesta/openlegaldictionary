---
title: Pais, Pays
letter: P
permalink: "/definitions/pais-pays.html"
body: Fr. The country; the neighborhood. A trial per pat's signifies a trial by the
  country; that is, by jury. An assurance by matter in pais is an assurance transacted
  between two or more private per-sons “Jn the country;” that is, upon the very spot
  to be transferred. Matter in pais signifies matter of fact, probably because matters
  of fact are triable by the country; i. c., by Jury; estoppels in pais are estoppels
  by conduct, as distinguished frpm estoppels by deed or by record
