---
title: Landmark
letter: L
permalink: "/definitions/bld-landmark.html"
body: A monument or erection set up on the boundary line of two adjoining estates,
  to fix such boundary. The removing of a landmark is a wrong for which an action
  lies
published_at: '2018-07-07'
source: Black's Law Dictionary 2nd Ed (1910)
