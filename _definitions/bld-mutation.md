title: Mutation
letter: M
permalink: "/definitions/bld-mutation.html"
body: In French law. This term is synonymous with “change,” and is es-pecially applied
  to designate the change which takes place in the property of a thing in its transmission
  from one person to an-other. Mutation, therefore, happens when the owner of the
  thiug sells, exchanges, or gives it. Merl. Repert
