---
title: Lend
letter: L
permalink: "/definitions/lend.html"
body: To part with a thing of value to another for a time fixed or indefinite, yet
  to have some time in ending, to be used or en-joyed by that other, the thing itself
  or the equivalent of it to be given back at the time fixed, or when lawfully asked
  for, with or without compensation for the use as may be agreed upon. Kent v. Quicksilver
  Min. Co., 78 N. Y. 177
published_at: '2018-07-07'
source: Black's Law Dictionary
layout: post
---
