---
title: Hypostasis
letter: H
permalink: "/definitions/bld-hypostasis.html"
body: In medical jurisprudence. (1) The morbid deposition of a sediment of any kind
  in the body. (2) A congestion or flushing of the blood vessels, as in varicose veins.
  Post-mortem hypostasis, a peculiar lividity of the cadaver
published_at: '2018-07-07'
source: Black's Law Dictionary 2nd Ed (1910)
