title: De Coronatore Eligendo
letter: D
permalink: "/definitions/bld-de-coronatore-eligendo.html"
body: Writ for electing a coroner. A writ issued to the sheriff in England, commanding
  him to pro-ceed to the election of a coroner, which is done in fuli county court,
  the freeholders being the electors. Sewell, Sheriffs, 372
