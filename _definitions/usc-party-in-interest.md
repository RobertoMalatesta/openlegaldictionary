---
title: Party in interest
letter: P
permalink: "/definitions/usc-party-in-interest.html"
body: A party who has standing to be heard by the court in a matter to be decided
  in the bankruptcy case. The debtor, U.S. trustee or bankruptcy administrator, case
  trustee, and creditors are parties in interest for most matters.
published_at: '2018-08-06'
source: US Courts Glossary
