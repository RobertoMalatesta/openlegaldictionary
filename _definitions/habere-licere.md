title: Habere Licere
permalink: "/definitions/habere-licere.html"
body: Lat. In Roman law. To allow [one] to have [possession.] This phrase denoted
  the duty of the seller of property to allow the purchaser to have the possession
  and enjoyment. For a breach of this duty, an actio ex empto might be main-tained
