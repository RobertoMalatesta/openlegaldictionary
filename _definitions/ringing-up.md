---
title: Ringing Up
permalink: "/definitions/ringing-up.html"
body: A custom among commission merchants and brokers, (not unlike the clearing-house
  system) by wbich they ex-change contracts for sale against contracts for purchase,
  or reciprocally cancel such con-tracts, adjust differences of price between themselves,
  and surrender margins. See (ward v. Vosburgh (C. C.) 31 Fed. 12; williar *v. Irwin,
  30 Fed. Cas. 38; Pardridge v. Cut-Jer, 68 111. App. 573; Samuels v. oilver, 130
  111. 73, 22 N. E. 499
