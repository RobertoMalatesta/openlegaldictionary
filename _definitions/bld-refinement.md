---
title: Refinement
letter: R
permalink: "/definitions/bld-refinement.html"
body: A term sometimes em-ployed to describe verbiage inserted in a pleading or indictment,
  over and above what is necessary to he set forth; or an objection to a plea or indictment
  on the ground of its failing to include such superfluous matter. See State v. Galllmon,
  24 N. C. 377; State v. Peak, 130 N. C. 711, 41 S. E. 887
published_at: '2018-07-07'
source: Black's Law Dictionary 2nd Ed (1910)
layout: post
---
