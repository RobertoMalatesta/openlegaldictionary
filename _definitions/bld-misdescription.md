---
title: Misdescription
letter: M
permalink: "/definitions/bld-misdescription.html"
body: An error or falsity in the description of the subject-matter of a contract which
  deceives one of the parties to his injury, or is misleading in a material or substantial
  point

source: Black's Law Dictionary 2nd Ed (1910)
