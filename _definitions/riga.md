title: Riga
permalink: "/definitions/riga.html"
body: In old European law. A species of service and tribute rendered to their lords
  by agricultural tenants. Supposed by Spel-man to be derived from the name of a certain
  portion of land, called, in England, a “rig" or “ridge,” an elevated piece of ground,
  formed out of several furrows. Burrill
