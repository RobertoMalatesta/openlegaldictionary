---
title: Pollards
letter: P
permalink: "/definitions/bld-pollards.html"
body: A foreign coin of base met-al, prohibited by St. 27 Edw. I. c. 3, from being
  brought into the realm, on pain of for-feiture of life and goods. 4 Bl. Comm. 98.
  It was computed at two pollards for a sterling or penny. Dyer, 82b
published_at: '2018-07-07'
source: Black's Law Dictionary 2nd Ed (1910)
layout: post
---
