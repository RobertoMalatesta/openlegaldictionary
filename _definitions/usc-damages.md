---
title: Damages
letter: D
permalink: "/definitions/usc-damages.html"
body: Money that a defendant pays a plaintiff in a civil case if the plaintiff has
  won. Damages may be compensatory (for loss or injury) or punitive (to punish and
  deter future misconduct).
