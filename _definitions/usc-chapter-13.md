---
title: Chapter 13
letter: C
permalink: "/definitions/usc-chapter-13.html"
body: The chapter of the Bankruptcy Code providing for the adjustment of debts of
  an individual with regular income, often referred to as a "wage-earner" plan. Chapter
  13 allows a debtor to keep property and use his or her disposable income to pay
  debts over time, usually three to five years.
