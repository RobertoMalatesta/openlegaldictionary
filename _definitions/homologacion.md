---
title: Homologacion
letter: H
permalink: "/definitions/homologacion.html"
body: In Spanish law. The tacit consent and approval inferred by law from the omission
  of the parties, for the space of ten days, to complain of the sen-tences of arbitrators,
  appointment of syndics, or assignees of insolvents, settlements of successions,
  etc. Also the approval given by the judge of certain acts and agreements for the
  purpose of rendering them more bind-ing and executory. Escrlche
