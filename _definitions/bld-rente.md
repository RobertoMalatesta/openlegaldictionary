  capital or of an immovable allenated. The coustitution of rente, is a contract by
  which one of the parties lends to the other a capital which he agrees not to recall,
  in con-slderatlon of the borrower’s paying an an-nual interest. It is this Interest
  which is called “rente." Duverger. The word is therefore nearly synonymous with
