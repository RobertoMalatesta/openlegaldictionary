---
title: Tacit
letter: T
permalink: "/definitions/bld-tacit.html"
body: Silent; not expressed; Implied or Inferred; manifested by the refraining from
  contradiction or objection; Inferred from the situation nnd circumstances, in the
  absence of express matter. Thus, tacit con-sent is consent Inferred from the fact
  that the party kept silence when he had an opportunity to forbid or refuse
published_at: '2018-07-07'
source: Black's Law Dictionary 2nd Ed (1910)
layout: post
---
