---
title: Misadventure
letter: M
permalink: "/definitions/bld-misadventure.html"
body: A mischance or accident; a casualty caused by the act of one person and indicting
  injury upon another. Homicide “by misadventure” is where n man, doing a lawful act,
  without any Intention of hurt, unfortunately kills another. 4 Bl. Comm. 182; williamson
  v. State, 2 Ohio Cir. Ct. R. 292; Johnson v. State, 94 Ala. 35, 10 South. 667
published_at: '2018-07-07'
source: Black's Law Dictionary 2nd Ed (1910)
layout: post
