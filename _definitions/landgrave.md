title: Landgrave
permalink: "/definitions/landgrave.html"
body: A name formerly given to those who executed justice on behalf of the German
  emperors, with regard to the internal policy of the country. It was ap* plied, by
  way of eminence, to those sovereign princes of the empire who possessed by inheritance
  certain estates called “land-gravates,” ot which they received investiture from
  the emperor. Enc. Lond
