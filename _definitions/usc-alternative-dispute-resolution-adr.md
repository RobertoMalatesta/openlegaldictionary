title: Alternative dispute resolution ADR
letter: A
permalink: "/definitions/usc-alternative-dispute-resolution-adr.html"
body: A procedure for settling a dispute outside the courtroom. Most forms of ADR
  are not binding, and involve referral of the case to a neutral party such as an
  arbitrator or mediator.
