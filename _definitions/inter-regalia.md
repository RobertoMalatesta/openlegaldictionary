---
title: Inter Regalia
letter: I
permalink: "/definitions/inter-regalia.html"
body: In English law. Among the things belonging to the sovereign. Among these are
  rights of salmon fishing, mines of gold and silver, forests, forfeitures, casualties
  of superiority, etc., which are called “regalia minora” and may be conveyed to a
  subject. The regalia majora include the several branches of the royal pre-rogative,
  w'hich are inseparable from the person of the sovereign. Tray. Lat. Max
published_at: '2018-07-07'
source: Black's Law Dictionary
