---
title: Ligan, Lagan
letter: L
permalink: "/definitions/bld-ligan-lagan.html"
body: Goods cast Into the sea tied to a buoy, so that they may be found again by the
  owners, are so denominated. when goods are cast into the sea in storms or shipwrecks,
  and remain there, without coming to land, they are distinguish-ed by the barbarous
  names of “Jetsam," “flotsam," and ‘ligan.” 5* Coke, 108; Harg. State Tr. 48; 1 Bl.
  Comm. 292
published_at: '2018-07-07'
source: Black's Law Dictionary 2nd Ed (1910)
