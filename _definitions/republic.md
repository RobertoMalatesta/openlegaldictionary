---
title: Republic
letter: R
permalink: "/definitions/republic.html"
body: A commonwealth; a form of government which derives all Its powers dlrectly or
  indirectly from the general body of citizens, and in which the executive power is
  lodged in officers chosen by and represent-ing the people, and holding office for
  a limited period, or at most during good behavior or at the pleasure of the people,
  and in which the legislative power may be (and in modern republics is) intrusted
  to a representative assembly. See Federalist, No. 39; Re-public of Mexico v. De
  Arangoiz, 5 Duer (N. Y.) 636; State v. Harris, 2 Bailey (S. C.) 599
