---
title: Steelbow Goods
letter: S
permalink: "/definitions/steelbow-goods.html"
body: In Scotch law. Corns, cattle, straw, and implements of hus-bandry delivered
  by a landlord to his tenant, by which the tenant is enabled to stock and labor the
  farm; in consideration of which he becomes bound to return articles equal in quantity
  and quality, at the expiry of the lease. Bell
