title: Littoral
letter: L
permalink: "/definitions/bld-littoral.html"
body: Belonging to the shore, as of seas and great lakes, webster. Corre-sponding
  to riparian proprietors on a stream or small pond are littoral proprietors on a
  sea or lake. But “riparian” is also used co-extenslvely with “littoral.” Commonwealth
  v. Alger, 7 Cush. (Mass.) 94. See Boston v. Lecraw, 17 How. 426, 15 L. Ed. 118
