---
title: Chapter 11
letter: C
permalink: "/definitions/usc-chapter-11.html"
body: A reorganization bankruptcy, usually involving a corporation or partnership.
  A Chapter 11 debtor usually proposes a plan of reorganization to keep its business
  alive and pay creditors over time. Individuals or people in business can also seek
  relief in Chapter 11.
published_at: '2018-08-06'
source: US Courts Glossary
