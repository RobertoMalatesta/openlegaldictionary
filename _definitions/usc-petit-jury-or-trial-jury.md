---
title: Petit jury or trial jury
letter: P
permalink: "/definitions/usc-petit-jury-or-trial-jury.html"
body: A group of citizens who hear the evidence presented by both sides at trial and
  determine the facts in dispute. Federal criminal juries consist of 12 persons. Federal
  civil juries consist of at least six persons.

source: US Courts Glossary
