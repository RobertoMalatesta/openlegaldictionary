---
title: Pending
letter: P
permalink: "/definitions/bld-pending.html"
body: Begun, hut not yet completed ; unsettled; undetermined; in process of settlement
  or adjustment. Thus, an action or suit is said to be “pending” from its inception
  until the rendition of final judg-ment. wentworth v. Farmington, 48 N. H. 210; Manney
  v. Pemberton, 75 N. C. 221; Ex parte Munford, 57 Mo. 603
published_at: '2018-07-07'
source: Black's Law Dictionary 2nd Ed (1910)
