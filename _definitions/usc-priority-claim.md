---
title: Priority claim
letter: P
permalink: "/definitions/usc-priority-claim.html"
body: An unsecured claim that is entitled to be paid ahead of other unsecured claims
  that are not entitled to priority status. Priority refers to the order in which
  these unsecured claims are to be paid.
published_at: '2018-08-06'
source: US Courts Glossary
layout: post
---
