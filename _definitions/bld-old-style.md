---
title: Old Style
letter: O
permalink: "/definitions/bld-old-style.html"
body: The ancient calendar or method of reckoning time, whereby the year commenced
  on March 25th. It was super-seded by the new style (that now in use) in most countries
  of Europe in 1582 and in England in 1752

source: Black's Law Dictionary 2nd Ed (1910)
