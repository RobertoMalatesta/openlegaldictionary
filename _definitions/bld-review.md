---
title: Review
letter: R
permalink: "/definitions/bld-review.html"
body: A reconsideration; second; view or examination; revision; consideration for
  purposes of correction. Used especially of the examination of a canse hy an appel-late
  court, and of a second investigation of a proposed public road by a Jury of viewers.
  See weehawken wharf Co. v. Knickerbocker Coal Co., 25 Misc. Rep. 300, 54 N. Y. Supp
  566 ; State v. Main, 69 Conn. 123, 37 Atl. 80, 36 L. R. A. 623, 61 Am. St. Rep.
  30
