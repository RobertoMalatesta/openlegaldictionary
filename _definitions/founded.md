title: Founded
permalink: "/definitions/founded.html"
body: Based upon; arising from, growing out of, or resting upon; as in the expressions
  “founded in fraud,” “founded on a consideration,” “founded on contract," and the
  like. See In re Grant Shoe Co., 130 Fed. 881, 06 C. C. A. 78; State v. Morgan, 40
  Conn. 46; Palmer v. Preston, 45 Vt. 158, 12 Am. Rep. 191; Steele v. Hoe, 14 Adol.
  & El. 431; In re Morales (D. C.) 105 Fed. 761
published_at: '2018-07-07'
