title: Leviable
letter: L
permalink: "/definitions/bld-leviable.html"
body: That which may be levied. That which is a proper or permissible sub-Ject for
  a levy; as, a “leviable interest” in land. See Bray v. Ragsdale, 53 Mo. 172

source: Black's Law Dictionary 2nd Ed (1910)
