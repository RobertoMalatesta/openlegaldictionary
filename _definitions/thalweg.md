---
title: Thalweg
letter: T
permalink: "/definitions/thalweg.html"
body: Germ.. A term used in topography to designate a line representing the deepest
  part of a continuous depression in the surface, such as a watercourse; hence the
  middle of the deepest part of the chan-nel of a river or other stream. See Iowa
  v. Illinois, 147 U. S. 1, 13 Sup. Ct 239, 37 L. Ed. 55; Keokuk & H. Bridge Co. v.
  People, 145 111. 596, 34 N. E. 482
published_at: '2018-07-07'
source: Black's Law Dictionary
