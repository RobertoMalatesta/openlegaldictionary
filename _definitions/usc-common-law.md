---
title: Common law
letter: C
permalink: "/definitions/usc-common-law.html"
body: The legal system that originated in England and is now in use in the United
  States, which relies on the articulation of legal principles in a historical succession
  of judicial decisions. Common law principles can be changed by legislation.
published_at: '2018-08-06'
source: US Courts Glossary
