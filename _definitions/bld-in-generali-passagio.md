---
title: In Generali Passagio
letter: I
permalink: "/definitions/bld-in-generali-passagio.html"
body: In the general passage; that is, on the journey to Palestine with the general
  company or body of Crusaders. This term was of frequent occurrence in the old law
  of essoins, as a means of accounting for the absence of the party, and was distinguished
  from simplex possagi-urn, which meant that he was performing a pilgrimage to the
  Holy Land alone
published_at: '2018-07-07'
source: Black's Law Dictionary 2nd Ed (1910)
