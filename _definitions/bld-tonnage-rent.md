---
title: Tonnage-Rent
letter: T
permalink: "/definitions/bld-tonnage-rent.html"
body: when the rent re-served by a mining lease or the like consists of a royalty
  on every ton of minerals gotten in the mine, it is often called a “tonnage-rent”
  There is generally a dead rent in ad-dltion. Sweet
published_at: '2018-07-07'
source: Black's Law Dictionary 2nd Ed (1910)
