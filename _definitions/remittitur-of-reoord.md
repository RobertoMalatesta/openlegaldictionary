---
title: Remittitur Of Reoord
letter: R
permalink: "/definitions/remittitur-of-reoord.html"
body: The returning or sending back by a court of appeal of the record and proceedings
  in a cause, after its decision thereon, to the court whence the appeal came, in
  order that the cause may be tried anew, (where it is so ordered,) or that judgment
  may be entered in accordance with the decision on appeal, or execution be issued,
  or any other necessary action be taken in the court below
published_at: '2018-07-07'
layout: post
---
