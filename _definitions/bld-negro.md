title: Negro
letter: N
permalink: "/definitions/bld-negro.html"
body: The word, “negro" means a black man, one descended from the African race, and
  does not commonly include a mulatto. Felix v. State, 18 Ala. 720. But the laws of
  the different states are not uniform in thia respect, some including in the description
  “negro” one who has one-eighth or more of African blood
published_at: '2018-07-07'
source: Black's Law Dictionary 2nd Ed (1910)
