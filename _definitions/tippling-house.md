  be drunk on the premises, and where men resort for drinking purposes. See Leesburg
  v. Putnam, 103 Ga. 110, 29 S. E. 602; Morrison v. Com., 7 Dana (Ky.) 219; Patten
  v. Centralia, 47 111. 370; Hussey v. State, 69 Ga. 58; Emporia v. Volmer, 12 Kan.
