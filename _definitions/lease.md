title: Lease
letter: L
permalink: "/definitions/lease.html"
body: A conveyance of lands or fene-ments to a person for life, for a term of years,
  or at will, in consideration of a return of rent or some other recompense. The person
  wbo so conveys such lands or tenements is termed the "lessor,” and the person to
  whom they are conveyed, the “lessee;” and when the lessor so conveys lands or tenements
  to a lessee, he is said to lease, demise, or let them. 4 Cruise, Dig. 58
