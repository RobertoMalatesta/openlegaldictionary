body: As used in legal phrases, thls word means attributed vicariously; that is, an
  act, fact, or quality is said to be “imput-ed” to a person when it is ascribed or
  charged to him, not because he is personally cognizant of it or responsible for
  it, but because another person is, over whom he has control or for whose acts or
  knowledge he is re-sponsible
