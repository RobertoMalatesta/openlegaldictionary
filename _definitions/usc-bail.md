---
title: Bail
letter: B
permalink: "/definitions/usc-bail.html"
body: The release, prior to trial, of a person accused of a crime, under specified
  conditions designed to assure that person's appearance in court when required. Also
  can refer to the amount of bond money posted as a financial condition of pretrial
  release.
published_at: '2018-08-06'
source: US Courts Glossary
layout: post
---
