---
title: Gout
letter: G
permalink: "/definitions/bld-gout.html"
body: In medical jurisprudence. An inflammation of the fibrous nnd ligamentous parts
  of the joints, characterized or caused by an excess of uric acid in the blood; usually,
  but not invariably, occurring in the joints of the feet, and then specifically called
  “poda-gra.”

source: Black's Law Dictionary 2nd Ed (1910)
