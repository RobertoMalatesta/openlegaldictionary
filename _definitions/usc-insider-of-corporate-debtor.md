---
title: Insider of corporate debtor
letter: I
permalink: "/definitions/usc-insider-of-corporate-debtor.html"
body: A director, officer, or person in control of the debtor; a partnership in which
  the debtor is a general partner; a general partner of the debtor; or a relative
  of a general partner, director, officer, or person in control of the debtor.
published_at: '2018-08-06'
source: US Courts Glossary
layout: post
---
