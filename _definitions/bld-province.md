title: Province
letter: P
permalink: "/definitions/bld-province.html"
body: E. Sometimes this signifies the district into which a country lias been divid
  ed; as, the province of Canterbury, in Eng-land; the province of Languedoc, in France.
  Sometimes it means a dependency or colony, as, the province of New Brunswick. It
  is sometimes used figuratively to signify power or authority; as, it is the province
  of the court to judge of the law; that of the jury to decide on the facts. 1 Bl.
  Comm. Ill; Tomlins
