---
title: Pot-De-Vin
permalink: "/definitions/pot-de-vin.html"
body: 'In French law. A sum of money frequently paid, at the moment of entering into
  a contract, beyond the price agreed upon. It differs from arrha, in this: that it
  is no part of the price of the thing sold, and that the person who lias received
  it cannot, by returning double the amount, or the other party by losing what he
  has paid, rescind the contract. 18 Toullier, uo. 52'
