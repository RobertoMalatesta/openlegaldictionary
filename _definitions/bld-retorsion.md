---
title: Retorsion
letter: R
permalink: "/definitions/bld-retorsion.html"
body: In international law. A species of retaliation, which takes place where a government,
  whose citizens are subjected to severe and stringent regulation or harsh treatment
  by a foreign government, employs measures of equal severity and harshness upon the
  subjects of the latter government found within its dominions. See Vattel, lib. 2,
  c. 18, S 341
published_at: '2018-07-07'
source: Black's Law Dictionary 2nd Ed (1910)
layout: post
---
