---
title: Prohibited Degrees
letter: P
permalink: "/definitions/bld-prohibited-degrees.html"
body: Those degrees of relationship by consanguinity which are so close that marriage
  between persons related to each other In any of such degrees Ib forbidden by law.
  See State v. Guiton, 51 La. Ann. 155, 24 South. 784

source: Black's Law Dictionary 2nd Ed (1910)
