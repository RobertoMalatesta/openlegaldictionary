title: Quare Ejecit Infra Terminum
letter: Q
permalink: "/definitions/bld-quare-ejecit-infra-terminum.html"
body: wherefore he ejected within the term. In old practice. A writ which lay for
  a lessee where he was ejected before the expiration of hls term, in cases where
  the wrong-doer or ejector was not himself in possession of the lands, but his feoffee
  or another claiming under him. 3 Bl. Comm. 199, 206; Reg. orig. 227; Fltzh. Nat
  Brev. 197 S.
