body: The name of rebels is given to all subjects who unjustly take up arms against
  the ruler of the society, [or the law-ful and constitutional government,] whether
  their view be to deprive him of the supreme authority or to resist his lawful commands
  in some particular instance, nnd to impose conditions on him. Vatt. Law Nat bk.
  3, t 288
