---
title: Disposable income
letter: D
permalink: "/definitions/usc-disposable-income.html"
body: Income not reasonably necessary for the maintenance or support of the debtor
  or dependents. If the debtor operates a business, disposable income is defined as
  those amounts over and above what is necessary for the payment of ordinary operating
  expenses.
published_at: '2018-08-06'
source: US Courts Glossary
layout: post
---
