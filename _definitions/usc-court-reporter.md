---
title: Court reporter
letter: C
permalink: "/definitions/usc-court-reporter.html"
body: A person who makes a word-for-word record of what is said in court, generally
  by using a stenographic machine, shorthand or audio recording, and then produces
  a transcript of the proceedings upon request.
published_at: '2018-08-06'
source: US Courts Glossary
layout: post
---
