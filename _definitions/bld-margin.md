---
title: Margin
letter: M
permalink: "/definitions/bld-margin.html"
body: 1. The edge or border; the edge of a body of water where it meets the land.
  As applied to a boundary line of land, the “margin” of a river, creek, or other
  water-course means the center of the stream. Ez parte Jennings, 6 Cow. (N. Y.) 527,
  16 Am. Dec. 447; Varick v. Smith, 9 Paige (N. ¥.) 551. But in the case of a lake,
  bay, or natural pond, tbe “margin” means the line where land and water meet. Fowler
  v. Vree-land, 44 N. J. Eq. 268, 14 Atl. 116; Lem-beck v. Andrews, 47 ohio St. 336,
  24 N. E. 686, 8 L. R. A. 578
