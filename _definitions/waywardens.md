title: Waywardens
letter: W
permalink: "/definitions/waywardens.html"
body: The English highway acts provide that In every parish forming part of a highway
  district there shall an-nuaily be elected one or more waywardens. The waywardens
  so elected, and the justices for the county residing within the district, form the
  highway board for the district. Each waywarden also represents his parish in regard
  to the levying of the highway rates, and in questions arising concerning the liability
  of his parish to repairs, etc. Sweet
