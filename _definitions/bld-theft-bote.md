---
title: Theft-Bote
letter: T
permalink: "/definitions/bld-theft-bote.html"
body: The offense committed by a party who, having been robbed and knowing the felon,
  takes back bis goods again, or receives other amends, upon an agreement not to prosecute.
  See Forshner v. whltcomb, 44 N. H. 16
published_at: '2018-07-07'
source: Black's Law Dictionary 2nd Ed (1910)
