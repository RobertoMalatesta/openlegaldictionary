title: Gavelet
letter: G
permalink: "/definitions/bld-gavelet.html"
body: An ancient and special kind of cessavit, used in Kent and London for the recovery
  of rent, obsolete. The statute of gavelet is 10 Edw. II. 2 Reeve, Eng. I4tw, c.
  12, p. 298. See Emig v. Cunningham, 62 Md. 460
