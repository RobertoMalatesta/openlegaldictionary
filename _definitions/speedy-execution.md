---
title: Speedy Execution
letter: S
permalink: "/definitions/speedy-execution.html"
body: An execution which, by the direction of the judge at nisi prius, issues forthwith,
  or on some early day fixed upon by the judge for that pur-pose after the trial of
  the action. Brown

source: Black's Law Dictionary
