---
title: Unscheduled debt
letter: U
permalink: "/definitions/usc-unscheduled-debt.html"
body: A debt that should have been listed by the debtor in the schedules filed with
  the court but was not. (Depending on the circumstances, an unscheduled debt may
  or may not be discharged.)
published_at: '2018-08-06'
source: US Courts Glossary
layout: post
---
