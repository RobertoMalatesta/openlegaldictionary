---
title: Writ Of Error
letter: W
permalink: "/definitions/bld-writ-of-error.html"
body: A writ issued from a court of appellate jurisdiction, directed to the judge
  or judges of a court of record, re-qulring them to remit to the appellate court
  the record of an action before them, in which a final judgment has been entered,
  in order that examination may be made of certain errors alleged to have been committed,
  and that the judgment may be reversed, corrected, or affirmed, as the case may require
published_at: '2018-07-07'
source: Black's Law Dictionary 2nd Ed (1910)
layout: post
---
