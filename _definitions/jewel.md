  etc., which are prepared to be worn. See Com. v. Stephens, 14 Pick. (Mass.) 373;
  Robbins v. Robertson (C. C.) 33 Fed. 710; Cavendish v. Cavendish, 1 Brown Ch. 409;
  Ramaley v. Leland, 43 N. Y. 541, 3 Am. Rep. 728; Gile v. Libby, 30 Barb. (N. Y.)
