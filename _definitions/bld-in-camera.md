---
title: In Camera
letter: I
permalink: "/definitions/bld-in-camera.html"
body: In chambers; in private. A cause is said to be heard in camera either when the
  hearing is had before the Judge in his private room or when all spectators are excluded
  from the court-room
published_at: '2018-07-07'
source: Black's Law Dictionary 2nd Ed (1910)
