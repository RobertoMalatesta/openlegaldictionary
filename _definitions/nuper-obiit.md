---
title: Nuper Obiit
permalink: "/definitions/nuper-obiit.html"
body: Lat. In practice. The name of a writ (now abolished) which, ln the English law,
  lay for a sister co-heiress dispossessed by her coparcener of lands and tenements
  whereof their father, brother, or any common ancestor died seised of an estate in
  fee-simple. Fltzh. Nat. Brev. 197
