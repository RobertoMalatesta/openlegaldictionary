---
title: Nolle Prosequi
letter: N
permalink: "/definitions/nolle-prosequi.html"
body: Lat in practice. A formal entry upon the record, by the plaintiff in a civil
  suit or the prosecuting officer in a criminal action, by which he de-clares that
  he “will no further prosecute” the case, either as to some of the counts, or some
  of the defendants, or altogether. State v. Primm, 61 Mo. 171; Com. v. Casey, 12
  Allen (Mass.) 214; Davenport v. Newton, 71 VL 11, 42 Atl. 1087
published_at: '2018-07-07'
source: Black's Law Dictionary
layout: post
---
