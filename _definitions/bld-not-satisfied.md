---
title: Not Satisfied
letter: N
permalink: "/definitions/bld-not-satisfied.html"
body: A return sometimes made by sheriffs or constables to a writ of execution; but
  it is not a technical formula, and is condemned by the courts as ambigu-ous and
  insufficient. See Martin v. Martin, 50 N. C. 346; Langford v. Few, 146 Mo. 142,
  47 S. W. 927, 69 Am. St Rep. 606; Merrick v. Carter, 205 IlL 73, 68 N. B. 750
published_at: '2018-07-07'
source: Black's Law Dictionary 2nd Ed (1910)
layout: post
---
