---
title: Tare
letter: T
permalink: "/definitions/tare.html"
body: A deficiency in the weight or quantity of merchandise by reason of the weight
  of the box, cask, bag, or other recep-tacle which contains it and is weighed with
  it. Also an allowance or abatement of a cer-lain weight or quantity which the seller
  makes to the buyer, on account of the weight df such box, cask, etc. Napier v. Barney,
  5 Blatchf. 191, 17 Fed. Cas. 1149. See Tbet
published_at: '2018-07-07'
source: Black's Law Dictionary
