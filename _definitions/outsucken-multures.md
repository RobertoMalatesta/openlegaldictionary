title: Outsucken Multures
permalink: "/definitions/outsucken-multures.html"
body: In Scotch law. out-town multures; multures, duties, or tolls paid by persons
  voluutarlly grinding corn at any mill to which they are not thirled, or hound by
  tenure. 1 Forb. lust. pt. 2, p. 140.
