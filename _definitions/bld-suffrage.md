---
title: Suffrage
letter: S
permalink: "/definitions/bld-suffrage.html"
body: A vote; the act of voting; the right or privilege of casting a vote at public
  elections. The last is the meaning of the term in such phrases as “the extension
  of the suffrage,” “universal suffrage.” etc. See Spitzer v. Fulton, 33 Misc. Rep.
  257, 68 N. Y. Supp. 660
published_at: '2018-07-07'
source: Black's Law Dictionary 2nd Ed (1910)
layout: post
---
