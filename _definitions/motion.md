---
title: Motion
permalink: "/definitions/motion.html"
body: In praotice. An occasional application to a court by the parties or their counsel,
  in order to obtain some rule or order, which becomes necessary either in the progress
  of a cause, or summarily and whol-ly unconnected with plenary proceedings. Citizens*
  SL R. Co. v. Reed, 28 Ind. App. 629, 63 N. E. 770; Low v. Cheney, 3 How. Prac. (N.
  V.) 287; People v. Ah Sam, 41 Cal. 645; In re Jetter, 78 N. Y. 601
