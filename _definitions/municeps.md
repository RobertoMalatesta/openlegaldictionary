title: Municeps
letter: M
permalink: "/definitions/municeps.html"
body: Lat in Roman law. A provincial person; a countryman. This was the designation
  of one born in the provinces or in a city politically connected with Rome, and who,
  having become a Roman citizen, was entitled to hold any offices at Rome except some
  of the highest. In the provinces the term seems to have been applied to the freemen
  of any city who were eligible to the munlclpal offices. Calvln
