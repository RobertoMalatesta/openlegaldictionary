---
title: Necessaries
letter: N
permalink: "/definitions/necessaries.html"
body: Things indispensable, or things proper and useful, for the sustenance of human
  life. This is a relative term, and its meaning will contract or expand according
  to the situation and social condition of the person referred to. Megraw v. woods,
  93 Mo. App. 647, 67 S. W. 709; warner v. Hefden, 28 wis. 517, 9 Am. Rep. 515; Artz
  v. Robertson, 50 111. App. 27; Conant v. Burnham, 133 Mass. 505, 43 Am. Rep. 532
published_at: '2018-07-07'
source: Black's Law Dictionary
layout: post
---
