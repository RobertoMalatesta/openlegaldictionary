---
title: Burden of proof
letter: B
permalink: "/definitions/usc-burden-of-proof.html"
body: The duty to prove disputed facts. In civil cases, a plaintiff generally has
  the burden of proving his or her case. In criminal cases, the government has the
  burden of proving the defendant's guilt. (See standard of proof.)
published_at: '2018-08-06'
source: US Courts Glossary
