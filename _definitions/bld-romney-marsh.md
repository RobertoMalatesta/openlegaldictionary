---
title: Romney Marsh
letter: R
permalink: "/definitions/bld-romney-marsh.html"
body: A tract of land in the county of Kent, England, containing twenty-four thousand
  acres, governed by cer-tain ancient and equitable laws of sewers, composed by Henry
  de Bathe, a venerable judge in the reign of king Henry III.; from which laws all
  commissioners of sewers in England may receive light and direction. 3 Bl. Comm.
  73, note t; 4 Inst 276
published_at: '2018-07-07'
source: Black's Law Dictionary 2nd Ed (1910)
