Lat. In English practice. A writ which issues to restrain a person from leaving the kingdom. It was formerly used for political purposes, but ls now only resorted to in equity when the de-fendant is about to leave the kingdom; lt is only In cases where the intention of the par-ty to leave can be shown that the writ is granted
