Untrue; erroneous; deceitful; contrived or calculated to deceive and in-jure. Unlawful. Iu law, this word means something more than untrue; lt means something designedly untrue and deceitful, and implies an intention to perpetrate some treachery or fraud. Hatcher v. Dunn, 102 Iowa, 411, 71 N. W. 343, 36 L. R. A. 689; Mason v. Association, 18 U. C. C. P. 19; Ratterman v. Ingalls, 48 ohio St. 468, 28 N. E. 168
