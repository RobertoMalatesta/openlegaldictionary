In English practice. A trial brought on by the def end-ant, in caseB where the plaintiff, after lssue joined, neglects to proceed to trial; so call-ed from a clause in the writ to the sheriff, which directs him, in case, two writs come to his hands, to execute but one of them. 3 Bl. Comm. 357
