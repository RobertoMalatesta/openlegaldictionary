Lat In old pleading, whereby he lost the company [of his wife.] A phrase used in the old declarations ln actions of trespass by a husband, for beating or ill using his wife, descriptive of tbe special damage he had sus-talned. 3 Bl. Comm. 140; Cro. Jac. 501, 538; Crocker v. Crocker (C. C.) 98 Fed. 703.
