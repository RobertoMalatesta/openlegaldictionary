Various, several, sundry; a collective term grouping a number of un-specified persons, objects, or acts. Com. v. Butts, 124 Mass. 452; State v. Hodgson, 66 Vt 134, 28 Atl. 1089; Munro v. Alaire, 2 Caines (N. Y.) 326
