Improved land is such as has been reclaimed, is used for the purpose of husbandry, and ls cultivated as such, whether the appropriation is for tillage, meadow, or pasture. “Improve" is synonymous with “cultivate.” Clark v. Phelps, 4 Cow. (N. Y.) 190
