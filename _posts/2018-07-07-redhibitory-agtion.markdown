In the civil law. An action for redhibition. An action to avoid a sale on account of some vice or defect ln the thing Bold, which renders its use Impossible, or so Inconvenient and imperfect that it must be supposed the buyer would not have purchased it had be known of the vice. Civ. Code La. art. 2520
